## ūüŹĀ What Is This?

**SPEC YOUR CAR** is a browser-based car-build randomizer that makes you spin 22 engineered wheels to assemble a complete vehicle from engine bay to bodywork ‚ÄĒ one component at a time.

It's not a simple dice roll. Every spin is **engineering-aware**: your chosen displacement filters out impossible configurations, your aspiration system changes what transmissions make sense, your drivetrain locks or unlocks certain differentials. Choose a CVT and RWD disappears. Pick a 660cc Kei engine and the V8 layouts vanish. That's how it works in real life. That's how it works here.

> *"The build doesn't care about your feelings. Spin the wheel."*

## ūüŹé Engineering Constraints

This is what separates SPEC YOUR CAR from a random number generator:

- **Displacement gates**: A 660cc engine eliminates V8, V10, V12, W12, W16 layouts. A 7.0L+ unlocks them all.
- **Aspiration gates**: Quad-turbo requires minimum 5.0L displacement AND a V8/V10/V12/W12/W16 layout.
- **FWD power cap**: Front-wheel drive is removed from the pool above 420hp ‚ÄĒ because physics.
- **CVT drivetrain lock**: CVT eliminates RWD, MR, and Drift-AWD. If you got a CVT, you're not doing donuts.
- **Brake HP gates**: Full drum brakes cap out at 100hp. Carbon ceramics require minimum 400hp to appear.
- **Suspension-stance links**: Hellaflush and static slam are unavailable on pushrod race geometry. FIA minimum height requires proper race suspension.
- **Diff-drivetrain logic**: Spool diffs only appear on RWD/MR. FWD filters out AYC and torque vectoring.
- **Wing-bodykit links**: RWB ducktail only available with RWB widebody. DRS wing needs serious aero kit.

## ūüéģ Features

### Auto-Spin Toggle
Hit the **‚ö° AUTO SPIN** button and the app takes the wheel (literally). It spins automatically, plays the full animation, waits on a `3 ‚Üí 2 ‚Üí 1` countdown, and advances to the next category ‚ÄĒ repeating until the phase ends. Toggle it off mid-sequence and it stops cleanly after the current spin. No re-spins, no race conditions.

### Tiered Win Sounds
Every spin result has a **tier-matched audio fanfare** built from Web Audio API synthesis:
- `F` ‚ÄĒ a single sad beep
- `D/C` ‚ÄĒ building short chord
- `B/A` ‚ÄĒ rising arpeggio
- `S` ‚ÄĒ full chord cascade
- `SS` ‚ÄĒ V8 exhaust pops + orchestral fanfare
- `SSS` ‚ÄĒ exhaust pops, rev limiter bounce, then a full reverb shimmer sequence

Plus: engine idle on spin start, turbo spool in aesthetics phase, gear shift click on Next Wheel.

### Clickable Progress Dots
The footer shows every wheel as a dot. **Completed dots are clickable** ‚ÄĒ open a review modal to re-read your past spin results. Cross-phase dots are locked until you reach that phase.

### Car Configuration Lab
After finishing your build, open the **‚öô Configuration Lab** ‚ÄĒ a free-build mode where you can manually set every single component and see live stats recalculate in real time:
- Estimated peak power (BHP)
- Estimated torque (lb-ft)
- 0‚Äď60 MPH estimate
- Top speed estimate
- Power-to-weight ratio
- Handling score (suspension + diff + brakes)
- Aero score (bodykit + wing)
- Style score (aesthetic tier average)
- Sound character description (engine layout √ó exhaust)

### Real Car Matching
The lab also has a **Find Matching Cars** algorithm that scores your configuration against a database of **38 real-world cars** ‚ÄĒ from the Toyota Corolla 1.8 CVT (tier F) to the Bugatti Chiron Super Sport (tier SSS). Cards are coloured by **enthusiast desirability tier**, not match percentage. A 90% match to a Jazz CVT is still grey. A 60% match to an R34 GT-R is still gold.

### Build Report
The final screen generates:
- A **performance codename** (`THE QUAD SNAIL KING`, `BUGATTI DROPOUT`, `THE TYRE MURDERER`)
- A **style vibe** (`RWB MONK`, `WOLF IN SHEEP'S CLOTHING`, `JDM PERFECTION`)
- Overall rating (D through SSS)
- Build class (Economy Special ‚Üí Hypercar Territory)
- Performance score, style score, estimated 0‚Äď60
- Full spec sheet of all 22 components coloured by tier
